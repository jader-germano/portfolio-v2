# Privacy Policy - JPG Labs Portfolio (LGPD Compliant)

## 1. Overview
Jader Philipe Germano ("JPG Labs") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD).

## 2. Information We Collect
- **Identity Data:** Email address (via GitHub, Google, or direct input).
- **Authentication Data:** Hashes of temporary auth keys and biometric verification metadata (stored locally on your device).
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, and device information for security auditing and VPS monitoring.

## 3. Purpose of Processing
We process your data for the following legitimate interests:
- **Authentication:** To verify your identity and grant access to the requested areas.
- **Service Security:** To prevent unauthorized access and monitor VPS health.
- **Pi Service Integrity:** To allow the `PI_AGENT_OS` to manage portfolio items and architectural snapshots autonomously.

## 4. User Rights (LGPD)
Under LGPD, you have the right to:
- Confirm the existence of data processing.
- Access your personal data.
- Correct incomplete or inaccurate data.
- Request the anonymization or deletion of unnecessary data.
- Revoke your consent at any time.

## 5. Data Retention
Identity and audit records are retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in Section 3, or to comply with legal and security obligations.

## 6. Biometric Data (Pi-Guard)
Our "Pi-Guard" system uses native device biometrics (TouchID/FaceID). JPG Labs **does not store** your actual biometric patterns on its servers. Only the success/failure result of the device-level authentication is transmitted to our backend to unlock session windows.

## 7. Contact Information
For any requests related to your data or this policy, please contact: `jader@jpglabs.com.br`.
